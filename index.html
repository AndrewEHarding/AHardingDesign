Greetings! I'm Andrew, an aspiring web/full-stack developer. After gaining an appreciation for coding in high school, I went on to get my Bachelor's Degree in Digital Media from Lyndon State College (now known as Northern Vermont University). In 2018, I went on to earn my Full Stack Web Developer Certification from the University of New Hampshire Coding Bootcamp. On this site, you can view my work and get in touch with me.
